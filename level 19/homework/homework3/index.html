About Football

Football, commonly known as soccer in some countries, is a popular team sport played internationally.

Basic Rules:

  1. The game consists of two teams of eleven players.
  2. The objective is to score more goals than the opposing team.
  3. Players can use any part of their body except the hands and arms.

Popular Competitions:

  • FIFA World Cup
  • UEFA Champions League
  • Copa America
